About Us

Vacation Vibes is new kind of travel-planning company that matches your travel goals with your vibe. Whether you prefer energetic nightlife or meditative hikes, we've got you covered. Vacation Vibes was founded as a response to one-size-fits-all travel planning that directs you to popular sites and destinations without taking into account your VIBE.
